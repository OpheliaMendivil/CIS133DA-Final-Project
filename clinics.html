Desert Star Family Planning

This is a place in which you can receive affirming healthcare among other things. Staff specializes in this area so you can expect to feel welcomed and comfortable.

Here you can expect to be offered things like wellness exams, Hormone Thereapy, STD testing and treatment, and HPV vaccines.

Plume

Plume was founded to make the process of obtaining hormones as simple, safe, convenient, and affirming as can be. At Plume, your identity is always respected, and our trans-led care team is here to support you every step of the way. They are a company for trans people, by trans people.
Here you can do everything online if you prefer not doing things in person!
